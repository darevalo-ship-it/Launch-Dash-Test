Data Sources

  • UOS schema — Unified Order Schema (Shopify + NetSuite): orders, units, revenue, customers
  • GA4_API.BASE_TRAFFIC — Google Analytics 4 sessions, transactions, revenue by channel
  • GA4_BQ_STG.BASE_LANDING_PAGE_STG2 — GA4 landing page sessions, CVR, revenue
  • UTS.PRODUCT_PAGE — Unified Traffic Schema: PDP views, add-to-cart, purchases by SKU
  • DRP.PRODUCT_AFFINITY_SETS — Product affinity / basket co-occurrence pairs
  • USS.ORDER_LINE_ITEMS — Recharge subscription orders
  • GSHEETS.SKU_LAUNCH_DAY_FORECAST — Daily unit plan from Google Sheets (falls back to the committed snapshot in config/plan_fallback.json when the schema isn't granted)

    Metric Definitions

    • Net Sales — SUM((price − discount/qty) × qty) on non-refunded line items (REFUND_FLAG = FALSE), converted to USD
    • Units Sold — SUM(quantity) on non-refunded line items
    • AOV — Net Sales ÷ Unique Orders
    • New vs Returning — a buyer is New if their first-ever UOS order is on/after the launch date
    • PDP CVR — Purchases ÷ Product Detail Views (UTS.PRODUCT_PAGE)
    • ATC Rate — Add-to-Cart ÷ PDP Views
    • Site CVR — Transactions ÷ Sessions (GA4, site-wide)
    • % to Plan — Actual Units ÷ SUM of daily planned units from launch date through the data cutoff (GSHEETS.SKU_LAUNCH_DAY_FORECAST)
    • New to Category — launch buyers with no prior purchase in the launch's category before launch date (full UOS order history, category via UOS.PRODUCTS.PRODUCT_TYPE)

    Notes & Caveats

    • Traffic data is site-wide, not launch-specific
    • PDP Health data is launch-SKU filtered via UTS.PRODUCT_PAGE
    • Cross-sell pairs sourced from DRP.PRODUCT_AFFINITY_SETS (same-cart basket analysis, launch SKU as primary)
    • Subscription counts include true Recharge subscription lines only (one-time purchases excluded); USS has no order dates, so totals are all-time
    • A source that isn't granted to the refresh's Snowflake user shows as empty with a note, and fills in automatically on the next refresh once access is granted
    • Figures can differ from earlier manual snapshots by a low single-digit % (USD conversion, first-order-based new/returning, plan window)
