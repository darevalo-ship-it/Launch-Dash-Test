Data Sources

  • UOS schema — Unified Order Schema (Shopify + NetSuite): orders, units, revenue, customers
  • GA4_API.BASE_TRAFFIC — Google Analytics 4 sessions, transactions, revenue by channel
  • GA4_BQ_STG.BASE_LANDING_PAGE_STG2 — GA4 landing page sessions, CVR, revenue
  • UTS.PRODUCT_PAGE — Unified Traffic Schema: PDP views, add-to-cart, purchases by SKU
  • DRP.PRODUCT_AFFINITY_SETS — Product affinity / basket co-occurrence pairs
  • USS.ORDER_LINE_ITEMS — Recharge subscription orders
  • GSHEETS.SKU_LAUNCH_DAY_FORECAST — Daily unit plan from Google Sheets

Data Freshness

This dashboard uses a static snapshot queried Jun 23, 2026 from Snowflake DAASITY_DB. Sales data covers through Jun 23 (yesterday). GA4 traffic data through Jun 21 (GA4 sync lag of ~2 days).

To refresh, re-query Snowflake and regenerate the HTML. Contact your Data & Analytics team.

Metric Definitions

  • Net Sales — SUM((price − discount/qty) × qty) on non-refunded line items
  • Units Sold — SUM(quantity) on non-refunded line items
  • AOV — Net Sales ÷ Unique Orders
  • PDP CVR — Purchases ÷ Product Detail Views (UTS.PRODUCT_PAGE)
  • ATC Rate — Add-to-Cart ÷ PDP Views
  • Site CVR — Transactions ÷ Sessions (GA4, site-wide)
  • % to Plan — Actual Units ÷ Planned Units from GSHEETS.SKU_LAUNCH_DAY_FORECAST

Notes & Caveats

  • Traffic data is site-wide, not launch-specific
  • PDP Health data is launch-SKU filtered via UTS.PRODUCT_PAGE
  • Cross-sell pairs sourced from DRP.PRODUCT_AFFINITY_SETS (same-order basket analysis)
  • New vs Returning split estimated from historical ratio applied to current totals
  • Subscription data from USS does not have order dates — all-time totals shown
